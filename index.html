Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense action game where you must fend off waves of monsters while surviving as long as possible. Equip your character with powerful weapons and fight in a variety of environments. Will you survive the monster onslaught?

How to Play

Use your mouse or keyboard to control your character and fight the incoming monsters. Collect items and upgrades to strengthen your chances of survival. The longer you survive, the more challenging the monsters become!

Game Features

  • Multiple levels and environments
  • Weapon upgrades and abilities
  • Challenging AI monsters
  • Playable on both desktop and mobile devices
